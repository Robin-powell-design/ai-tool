Proposed Tools

💬
01 — Feedback
Design Feedback Synthesiser
Problem

Feedback is slow and uncomfortable. Stakeholders rarely give honest opinions in group settings, and it's hard to find time for structured reviews. Things get missed.

Solution

A link-based tool that anyone in the company can open — anonymously if preferred — to leave structured design feedback. No meetings required.

How it works

Link shared via Slack → reviewer opens design in browser → guided by sliders and prompted questions → all responses collected and synthesised in one place for the design team to action.

Impact
Faster reviews Less missed feedback Documented decisions
⚙️
02 — Handoff
Design-to-Dev Spec Generator
Problem

Dev handoff is inconsistent and eats up time. Specs are missing, incomplete, or buried in Figma comments — leading to back-and-forth that slows shipping.

Solution

A tool that takes a Figma page and generates a full, clean spec document automatically — ready for devs to work from without chasing designers.

How it works

Upload Figma page → AI generates complete specs covering spacing, component states, interactions, edge cases, and accessibility notes. Exportable and shareable.

Impact
Faster handoff Fewer dev questions Consistent output
🔍
03 — Quality
AI-Powered Design QA Checker
Problem

Small UX issues consistently slip through — inconsistent spacing, accessibility failures, off-brand components. Manual QA is slow and still misses things.

Solution

AI scans designs before they leave the design phase and flags issues automatically, acting as a first-pass QA layer that catches the low-hanging problems.

How it works

Connect to Figma → AI audits layouts for spacing consistency, colour contrast, missing states, and component misuse → returns a prioritised issues list with context.

Impact
Higher quality baseline Fewer QA cycles Accessibility coverage
📊
04 — Insight
Support Ticket → Product Insight Engine
Problem

Support tickets are full of product intelligence that rarely reaches design or product. Patterns exist but no one has the bandwidth to read thousands of tickets.

Solution

AI continuously scans all support tickets and surfaces recurring UX issues, broken flows, and feature signals — mapped directly to product and design opportunities.

How it works

Connect to support platform → AI clusters and tags tickets by theme → extracts UX issues, broken flows, and feature requests → outputs a live dashboard of prioritised product insights.

Impact
Design from real pain Faster triage Untapped data surfaced
